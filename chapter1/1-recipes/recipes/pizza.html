Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Mozzarella
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Infuse the Oil: Mix olive oil with finely minced garlic in a small bowl. Let it sit for about 10 minutes so the garlic flavor fully infuses the oil.
  2. Prepare the Dough: Stretch your pizza dough onto a pizza tray (ideally one with holes for extra crispiness). Aim for an even thickness and leave a raised edge for the crust.
  3. Assemble the Pizza: Brush the garlic-infused oil evenly over the base. Sprinkle with grated parmesan, then cover generously with mozzarella, leaving the edges bare for a golden crust.
  4. Bake: Place in a preheated oven at 250°C (480°F). Bake for about 8 minutes, rotating the tray halfway through, until the cheese is melted, bubbly, and golden.
  5. Serve: Slice into wedges and enjoy hot, ideally with extra olive oil or fresh herbs on top.
